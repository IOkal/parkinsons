About us

We are a group of students who developed this software to tackle Parkinson's. A little background: One of our team member's grandfather has Parkinson's, but doctors cannot diagnose him definitively because the most common methods are only 53% accurate. After doing extensive research we found that there are 22 features in a patient's voice that help detect the onset of Parkinson's. Our algorithm uses a neural network to processes those features and make a prediction of whether or not the patient has Parkinson's. Our test set accuracy is 92%.
